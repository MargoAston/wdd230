Environment: Comfortable using a computer, uses email and web seraches extensively
Inhibiting Factors: staying up-to-date with community concerns public

Persona 2
Persona: small business owner
Fictional name: Matilda Watson
Job title/ major responsibilities: Owner / manage employees, serve customer inquiries, create marketing opportunities, oversee day to day operations
Demographics: 26 years old, married, mother of one child, has a BS in Electrical Engineering
Goals and tasks: maximize profits, grow business, achieve financial stability
Environment: Proficient computer user, uses email and web seraches extensively, uses professional platforms daily
