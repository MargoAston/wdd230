Lakeside Chamber

Site Plan

Margo Aston

WDD 230

Target Audience

Description

Our target audience is made up of businesses and individuals dedicated to the betterment of Lakeside, our children, and our environment.

Personas

Persona 1
Persona: public relations manager
Fictional name: William Jones
Job title/ major responsibilities: Casino Public Relations Manager / create press releases and increase brand awareness,
Demographics: 34 years old, single, father of two children, has an MBA
Goals and tasks: maintian a positive image for the Casino
Environment: Comfortable using a computer, uses email and web seraches extensively
Inhibiting Factors: staying up-to-date with community concerns public
Quote: “We care about strengthening Lakeside families and supporting locally owned Lakeside businesses.”
Persona 2
Persona: small business owner
Fictional name: Matilda Watson
Job title/ major responsibilities: Owner / manage employees, serve customer inquiries, create marketing opportunities, oversee day to day operations
Demographics: 26 years old, married, mother of one child, has a BS in Electrical Engineering
Goals and tasks: maximize profits, grow business, achieve financial stability
Environment: Proficient computer user, uses email and web seraches extensively, uses professional platforms daily
Inhibiting Factors: finding the right balance between cost-cutting and investing in the business
Quote: “You have to take risks to succeed.”

Scenarios

  • Does this organization address the homeless problem and other social issues?
  • How can I make connections with local businesses?
  • How do I become a member?
  • What marketing opportunities are currently being promoted?
  • Does this group provide information about community demographics?
