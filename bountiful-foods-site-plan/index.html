Overview

Purpose

Bountiful Foods is a community owned business specializing in organic, locally grown foods. Bountiful Foods has worked to strengthen the economic livelihoods of farmers since 2012 through partnerships with local markets, restaurants, and schools. Bountiful Foods' farm to school program promotes healthy eating through education and guided gardening. Bountiful Foods local food trucks are powered by recycled waste veggie oil. Bountiful Food Trucks distributes delicious delights to neighborhoods throughout southern California.
